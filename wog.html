Wog

A summary of my personal reflections

Quick presentation

I am currently taking a year long sabatical to work on a string of various reflections aiming to consolidate my general views of "important things".

I write my reflexions and used to have a regularly updated public version avaialble here (mainly intended as easy access for my friends and relatives). But I have not updated it recently and am nearing the end of the project and final writing phase anyway. Because I am not satisfied by anything I have in writing right now, I intend to leave this page as is until the end of the project. I expect a public version to be available by fall 2021.

wogian ?

"Wog" or "wogian" is a word I made up to refer to my current reflexions. Sometimes, I use "wogian" as an adjective to add in front of things I want to clarify without looking like I am trying to correct everyone. It's convenient for me, nothing more.
