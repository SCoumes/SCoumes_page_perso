Short presentation

I am a french 26 years old PhD student. My PhD takes place within the DIG team at Telecom Paris, under the direction of Fabian Suchanek. I care quite a lot about some AI related questions and about epistemology, but I also enjoy a healthy dose of mathematics and computer science.

Current work

My PhD focuses on the symbolic representation of knowledge and on formal reasoning. It is a part of the NoRDF project. My main contribution to date is a formal logic for contextual reasoning called Qiana (see my publications), which allows to use first-order theorem provers for contextual reasoning.
